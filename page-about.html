We are Bexar Communications

We have been in business since 1994, starting right from the beautiful Hill Country of Texas. We've grown a little since then, and we serve cities from Austin, San Antonio, Lubbock, Houston, Corpus Cristi, and more.

Telstar continues to work towards going national, because of the simplicity and possibilities brought with the very technology we sell, that allow us to be anywhere. So name the city you are in, no matter if it is down the street, or on the other side of the world; we are already there. We look forward to continuing our journey, with current and new found customers, throughout the years to come.

Not only do we have the knowledge, and experience to sell what people need, we have aspect, and work to gain a relationship, to guarantee customer satisfaction through family based values that go beyond the customers’ expectations to provide substantial service and customer care.
